# Product

## Register

product

## Users
JobSwipe moderators, administrators, and campus operators. They are responsible for reviewing active gigs, approving providers, handling reports/moderation cases, and keeping the student gig economy platform secure, fast, and healthy.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a high-density, real-time command cockpit for tracking campus gig activity, verifying service providers, and managing risk factors (such as no-shows and scam reports) to maintain high trust and transaction liquidity.

## Brand Personality
- **Sophisticated**: Expert-focused, clean, and intentional.
- **Accurate**: Precise data representation, high signal-to-noise ratio.
- **Vigilant**: Alert to risks, clear visual urgency without causing panic.

## Anti-references
- **Generic Cream-Colored SaaS**: Cliché warm-neutral card grids that feel slow and default.
- **Over-decorated Dashboard**: Rainbow-colored charts, excessive shadows, and large 32px rounded corners that waste screen space.
- **Low-contrast Text**: hard-to-read dark gray text on slightly darker backgrounds.

## Design Principles
- **Maximize Signal Density**: Prioritize information architecture and screen space over decorative whitespace. Let operators see the status of everything at a single glance.
- **Differentiated Urgency**: Use color, animations, and typography to highlight actionable states (e.g., active scam flags) while keeping normal metrics stable and unobtrusive.
- **Cyber-Ops Aesthetics**: Use a rich dark mode with a cohesive, dark slate/violet background, highly controlled glows, and clean structural grid lines to feel like a modern, professional command cockpit.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant contrast ratios for status indicators and data visualizations.
- Suppressed/reduced motion alternatives for animations.
- Clear typographic weight hierarchy to ensure scannability.
